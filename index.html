What We're Building

Proto Lounge LLC is an economic development and investment vehicle rooted in Walsenburg, Colorado. Our mission is to turn Main Street potential into real businesses — generating local revenue, creating community anchors, and attracting long-term investment to a city ready for its next chapter.

We are building for the long term.
