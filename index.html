Welcome to Proto Lounge

Proto Lounge is a thought in my head. I have an idea that 3D Printing, On Demand Manufacturing, is something that should be avialable to everyone. Print studios should be available to everyone and near your neighborhood. While the technology is still evolving and the processes improving, everyone should have access to the capability to create, learn and explore.

Proto Lounge is active on 3D Hubs If you need a print or would like to learn more drop me a note.
